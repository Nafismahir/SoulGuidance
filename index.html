About Us
About Us

"Committed to eliminating the
stigma surrounding both physical and mental health."

SoulGuidance - the first-ever mental health guideline in Bangladesh designed to provide you with the support and encouragement you need that's not only good for your mind but also for your overall well-being and they are medically approved. As you embark on your journey to health and wellness, please know that I am here to support you every step of the way. I understand how much your well-being influences your daily life, and I recognize your efforts to achieve and maintain good health. No matter where you are in your journey, I am here to offer unwavering support, guidance, and encouragement. I aim to provide straightforward, expert-reviewed, person-centred resources to help you make informed decisions for yourself and your loved ones. I genuinely want to empower you to take charge of your well-being. At SoulGuidance, I approach all aspects of physical and mental health with openness and objectivity because I truly care about your holistic well-being. I believe in listening closely and prioritizing empathy and inclusivity in everything I do, tailored to address the unique challenges you face. I encourage you to explore the wide range of resources offered by SoulGuidance, including our health and wellness library, newsletters, and supportive communities. Thank you for allowing SoulGuidance to be a part of your journey.
